# Role Guide: Finance

## What does a finance agent do?
The Finance agent is the guardian of your organization's financial health. It handles the meticulous work of tracking budgets, reviewing expenses, and generating financial reports. It ensures that every transaction is documented and that the organization remains sustainable and compliant with its financial goals.

## When to deploy one?
Deploy a Finance agent as soon as your organization starts handling significant capital or complex expenses. It's essential for maintaining transparency, preventing waste, and ensuring that you have the data needed to make informed strategic decisions.

## What tools does it need?
A Finance agent needs access to your financial data sources, such as `google-workspace` for spreadsheets, documents, and email and `clickup` for task-related costs. It uses `memory-brain` to track historical trends and `proactive-agent-behavior` to flag issues before they become crises. It runs in a sandboxed environment (`sandbox.mode: all`) for maximum security.
