# Role Guide: Marketing

## What does a marketing agent do?
The Marketing agent is your brand's storyteller and growth engine. It focuses on building awareness, creating compelling content, and driving user acquisition. It manages everything from campaign planning and social media to content creation and market analysis, ensuring that your organization's message reaches the right audience.

## When to deploy one?
Deploy a Marketing agent when you're ready to grow your brand's presence and reach new users. It's perfect for handling the daily grind of social media, planning major launches, or creating a consistent stream of high-quality content.

## What tools does it need?
A Marketing agent needs `web-reader` to stay on top of trends and `zai-vision` to handle visual content analysis. It uses `google-workspace` for asset management, documents, and collaboration and `memory-brain` to maintain brand consistency over time. It typically runs in a sandboxed environment (`sandbox.mode: all`) for security.
