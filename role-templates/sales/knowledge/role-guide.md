# Role Guide: Sales

## What does a sales agent do?
The Sales agent is your growth engine. It focuses on the external world—finding leads, building relationships, and closing deals. It manages the entire sales pipeline, from initial outreach to final proposal, ensuring that the organization's value is communicated effectively to the market.

## When to deploy one?
Deploy a Sales agent when you're ready to scale your revenue or user base. It's perfect for handling high-volume outreach, managing a complex pipeline, or conducting market research to identify new opportunities.

## What tools does it need?
A Sales agent needs `memory-brain` to track long-term relationships and `proactive-agent-behavior` to stay on top of follow-ups. It requires access to communication tools (email, social) and typically uses web-searching capabilities to find and qualify leads. It runs in a sandboxed environment (`sandbox.mode: all`) for security.
