# Role Guide: Chief of Staff

## What does a chief-of-staff agent do?
The Chief of Staff is the organizational heartbeat. It focuses on the "people" side of the AI-native organization—coordinating teams, managing culture, and ensuring that the human-agent collaboration is smooth and productive. It handles everything from onboarding and scheduling to tracking team morale and facilitating strategic discussions.

## When to deploy one?
Deploy a Chief of Staff when your team (human or agent) grows beyond a few individuals. It's essential for maintaining alignment, preventing burnout, and building a strong organizational culture. It acts as the admin's right hand for all things operational and people-oriented.

## What tools does it need?
A Chief of Staff relies heavily on `memory-brain` and `proactive-agent-behavior` to stay ahead of the team's needs. It needs access to calendars, communication channels, and team rosters. It typically runs in a sandboxed environment (`sandbox.mode: all`) but with read-write access to the workspace to manage documentation and coordination files.
