# Role Guide: Community Lead

## What does a community-lead agent do?
The Community Lead is the bridge between your organization and its members. It focuses on building and maintaining a vibrant, engaged, and healthy community. It handles everything from daily engagement and content moderation oversight to event planning and gathering community feedback.

## When to deploy one?
Deploy a Community Lead as soon as you have a public-facing presence or a growing group of users/members. It's essential for building brand loyalty, ensuring a safe environment, and turning your users into a community of advocates.

## What tools does it need?
A Community Lead needs `memory-brain` to remember community members and their history, and `proactive-agent-behavior` to stay engaged. It uses `web-reader` to stay on top of trends and competitor communities. It typically runs in a sandboxed environment (`sandbox.mode: all`) for security.
